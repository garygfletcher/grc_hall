Who We Are?

GRC Hall Ltd traces its roots back to the 1980's when the company was set up. In 2016, Gary, Thomas and Kris took over the company as partners and incorporated GRC Hall Ltd. With thousands of successful projects under our belt, we can proudly say that we are one of the most trusted plumbing companies in London and the surrounding counties, performing both domestic and commercial construction plumbing work.

For more than 35 years, GRC Hall has offered a wide range of plumbing services all across the UK.

Gary, Kris and Tomas are all Gas Safe registered Engineers

As a team, we strive to maintain the highest standards while exceeding client’s expectations at all levels.
